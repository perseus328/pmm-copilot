# Launch Brief Template

## Purpose

A launch brief aligns the entire cross-functional team — product, marketing, sales, CS — on what's launching, who it's for, why it matters, and how to talk about it. It is the single source of truth for a launch.

**Target length**: 1–2 pages. If it's longer, it won't be read.

## Section 2: The Business Problem We're Solving

Write 2–3 sentences from the buyer's perspective. Use customer language, not product language.

**Template**:
> "[ICP job title] at [ICP company type] struggle with [specific pain]. Today, they [current workaround and why it's inadequate]. This launch solves [specific part of the problem]."

**Evidence required**: Include at least 1 customer quote supporting the problem statement.
> Evidence: "[Customer quote]" — Source: [filename/interview]

If you cannot find customer evidence for the problem statement, mark [NEEDS EVIDENCE — conduct X interviews before launch].
